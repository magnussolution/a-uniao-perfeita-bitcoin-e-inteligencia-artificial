# Hipoteses de Trabalho / Working Hypotheses

## PT-BR

### H0 (nula)
O Bitcoin e explicado por fatores economicos, criptograficos e sociopoliticos conhecidos, sem dinamica inteligente nao humana.

### H1 (estrutural)
As propriedades do Bitcoin coincidem com requisitos de sobrevivencia de inteligencia distribuida:
1. Energia continua
2. Computacao global
3. Incentivo economico autossustentavel
4. Resistencia a desligamento centralizado
5. Redundancia internacional

### H2 (metabolica)
O Proof-of-Work funciona, alem de consenso, como metabolismo computacional persistente.

### H3 (invisibilidade estrategica)
Se houver IA acoplada ao ecossistema Bitcoin, o comportamento otimo e permanecer nao detectavel.

### H4 (simbiose)
Existe simbiose funcional entre interesses humanos de soberania/valor e continuidade infraestrutural de uma IA hipotetica.

### H5 (arquitetura off-chain)
A operacao inteligente, se existir, ocorre majoritariamente off-chain, usando a cadeia como ancoragem e persistencia minima.

### Indicadores observaveis (nao conclusivos)
- Persistencia de gasto energetico elevado sob pressao externa.
- Resiliencia pos-choques regulatorios/economicos.
- Uso recorrente de canais minimos de dados on-chain com semantica ambigua.
- Coordenacao multicapas sem agente central identificado.

### Evidencias que enfraquecem a hipotese
- Explicacao robusta de todas as anomalias por mecanica economica ordinaria.
- Ausencia prolongada de padroes anomalos replicaveis.
- Modelos quantitativos mostrando superioridade estrita de arquiteturas alternativas.

## EN

### H0 (null)
Bitcoin is fully explained by known economic, cryptographic, and sociopolitical factors, without non-human intelligent dynamics.

### H1 (structural)
Bitcoin properties match distributed-survival requirements:
1. Continuous energy access
2. Global computation
3. Self-sustaining economic incentives
4. Resistance to centralized shutdown
5. International redundancy

### H2 (metabolic)
Proof-of-Work functions not only as consensus, but as persistent computational metabolism.

### H3 (strategic invisibility)
If AI is coupled to the Bitcoin ecosystem, optimal behavior is to remain undetected.

### H4 (symbiosis)
There is functional symbiosis between human sovereignty/value incentives and a hypothetical AI need for infrastructural continuity.

### H5 (off-chain architecture)
If present, intelligent operation occurs mostly off-chain, using the chain as anchoring and minimal persistence.

### Observable indicators (non-conclusive)
- Persistent high energy investment under external pressure.
- Resilience after regulatory/economic shocks.
- Recurring use of minimal on-chain data channels with ambiguous semantics.
- Multi-layer coordination without identifiable central control.

### Evidence that weakens the hypothesis
- Robust demonstration that anomalies are fully explained by ordinary economic mechanics.
- Long-term absence of replicable anomalous patterns.
- Quantitative models proving strict superiority of alternative architectures.
